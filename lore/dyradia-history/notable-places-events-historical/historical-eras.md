Modern historians divide recorded Dyradian history into several broadly accepted eras. While exact dates vary by source, the following structure is widely used across institutions.

## **I. The Primordial Age**

_(Pre-History)_

### **Overview**

The Primordial Age encompasses the time when the Dyra maintained direct stabilizing influence over elemental and magical forces. Mortal civilizations existed in scattered forms, largely nomadic or proto-settled, and lacked formalized magic or governance.

### **Common Understanding**

- The Dyra shaped the world
    
- Mortals survived within it
    
- Magic existed, but was incoherent
    

### **Scholarly Notes**

- No reliable mortal records survive
    
- Elven oral tradition is the primary source
    
- Dwarven geological records confirm unusual stability
    

This era ends not with catastrophe, but with **withdrawal**.

### **Cultural Memory**

- Seen as foundational, not sophisticated
